Drop your 5 reference PDFs here before starting the backend. They get
auto-ingested into ChromaDB on startup (see main.py's startup_ingest()).

Suggested set (matches the pitch):
  1. AI Safety (e.g. an AI safety overview or Anthropic/OpenAI safety paper)
  2. Company Policy (your own or a sample AI usage policy doc)
  3. WHO Guidelines (e.g. "Ethics and governance of artificial intelligence
     for health" - WHO, freely downloadable from who.int)
  4. Government AI Rules (e.g. an EU AI Act summary or India's NITI Aayog
     "Responsible AI" strategy paper)
  5. NIST AI Risk Management Framework (freely downloadable PDF from
     nist.gov/itl/ai-risk-management-framework)

Any 5 PDFs work -- these just make the demo's "PDF Evidence Agent" citations
look authoritative and on-theme.
